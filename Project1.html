Web Site Critique

Website Reviewed: Trader Joe's - www.traderjoes.com

  1. My first impression of the site was that it's a little underwhelming, considering how successful and well know Trader Joe's is. Then again, it's nice and simple, and not gunked up with too much unneccessary UI.
  2. The site information is organized with a top menu and sections stacked down the page. You mostly scroll and click into categories.
  3. The structure makes sense and is easy to follow. I don’t feel lost when I click around.
  4. The graphics enhance the site because they make the food look appealing. The layout feels fun and on-brand.
  5. The home page loads fairly quickly. The load time feels reasonable for a modern website.
  6. The text is easy to read and the colors don’t hurt my eyes. The background helps the site feel warm, not distracting.
  7. Yes, there is a clear navigation bar at the top. It’s easy to find what I’m looking for.
  8. The same style and colors are used across the pages. It feels consistent.
  9. On smaller screens, the layout adjusts and still works well. Nothing important gets cut off.
  10. The site does a great job making grocery shopping feel fun and interesting, not boring.
